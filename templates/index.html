User Participation Notice

Welcome to our story-sharing platform! Before proceeding, please carefully read the following terms to understand your rights, our responsibilities, and how we protect your data:

1. Data Collection and Usage

• The personal information you provide (e.g., username, age, gender) and story content will be used to enable platform features, including displaying your stories, generating feedback, and storing them in your profile.

• We will only use your data with your consent and for specific, lawful purposes.

2. Data Privacy and Protection

• Your data will be securely processed and stored in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act (DPA) 2018.

• We employ robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

3. Your Rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: View the personal data we collect about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Update or correct your personal information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your account and stored data.
  • Right to Object: Opt out of specific uses of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request to export your data to another platform.
